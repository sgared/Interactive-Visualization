Interactive Scatter-Plot About Students Lifestyle

Welcome to the Interactive Scatterplot Visualization. This site enables you to explore relationships between different lifestyle attributes, such as study hours, extracurricular activities, sleep hours, and GPA. Use the dropdown menus above the chart to select variables for the X and Y axes.

The scatterplot dynamically updates based on your selections, allowing you to observe patterns and correlations in the dataset. Hover over points on the scatterplot to view detailed information about each data point. Gridlines are included for better readability.
